I don't do much cooking, but when I do cook I usually make this simple and delicious pancake recipe

  • prep: 5 min
  • cook: 15 min
  • total: 20 min
  • servings: 8

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour:$3.69
  • Baking powder: $1.89
  • Salt: $1.99
  • White sugar: $16.99
  • Milk: $3.99
  • Eggs: $2.26
  • Butter: $2.29

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, sift together 1 1/2 cups of the flour, 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder, 1 teaspoon of salt and 1 tablespoon sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in 1 1/4 cups of the milk, 1 egg and 3 tablespoons of the melted butter; mix until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot. Pancake
